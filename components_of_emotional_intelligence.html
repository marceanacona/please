Emotional Intelligence is key to navigating both personal and professional environments. It starts with Self- Awareness , which involves recognizing and understanding your own emotions. Following this, Self-Regulation is the ability to manage and control emotional reactions, especially in challenging situations. Equally important is Empathy, the capacity to understand and share the feelings of others. Relationship Management comes into play when building strong, healthy interactions with people around you. Finally, Motivation drives individuals to stay focused on their goals , even when faced with obstacles, reflecting a high level of emotional intelligence.
